# Usage Guide

This guide provides instructions on how to use the Kotlin Health Monitor.

## Main Dashboard

1. **Access the Main Dashboard:**
   - Open the app on your Android device.
   - Use the navigation menu to access different sections of the application.

## Uploading and Monitoring Models

1. **Upload Health Data:**
   - Navigate to the "Upload Model" section.
   - Click the "Choose File" button and select your health data file.
   - Click the "Upload" button to upload the data.

2. **Monitor Health Data:**
   - Navigate to the "Monitor Models" section.
   - View the list of uploaded health data and their status.
   - Each entry shows the data's upload status and other relevant information.

## Viewing Health Metrics

1. **Access Health Metrics:**
   - Navigate to the "Health Metrics" section.
   - View real-time health metrics such as heart rate, sleep hours, and steps.

2. **Personalized Recommendations:**
   - Navigate to the "Recommendations" section.
   - View personalized health recommendations based on your data.

For more detailed instructions and troubleshooting, refer to the [FAQ](#faq) section below.

## FAQ

### How do I reset the data?
   - To reset the data, you can re-upload new health data through the "Upload Model" section.

### What types of health data are supported?
   - Currently, the platform supports data from popular wearable devices such as Google Fit and Fitbit.
